No Scroll Extension

A No Scroll Extension is a browser extension designed to prevent users from scrolling on websites, including platforms like YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels. It essentially disables the scroll functionality on the page, forcing users to stay on the current visible content, without the ability to scroll further down or horizontally.

Key Features:

  • Block Scrolling: Disables mouse wheel scrolling, touchpad gestures, and keyboard arrow keys to prevent vertical or horizontal scrolling.
  • Customizable Settings: Enables or disables scrolling restrictions on specific websites or pages like YouTube Shorts or Instagram Reels.
  • Productivity & Focus: Helps users stay focused and prevent mindless browsing or excessive consumption of short-form content.
  • Simple User Interface: Offers easy-to-use controls with a toggle for scroll lock on/off.
  • Content Limitation: Useful for limiting engagement with platforms designed for endless scrolling.

Use Cases:

  • Focus Mode: Avoid distractions while working or studying.
  • Digital Well-being: Reduce time spent on platforms encouraging continuous consumption of content.
  • Parental Control: Restrict scrolling on specific social media sites to manage kids' screen time.

In essence, the No Scroll Extension is a tool for users looking to regain control over their digital habits and reduce time spent scrolling through endless content on social media and video platforms.
